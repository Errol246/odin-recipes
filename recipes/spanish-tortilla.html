Tortilla de Patatas

Home

Description

The Spanish tortilla, known as tortilla de patatas in the vernacular, is a Spanish potato omelette. It is an incredibly simple recipe with few ingredients, but it makes for a hearty and traditional, delicious Spanish dish that is well loved by its inhabitants.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut your potatoes into very thin slices, preferably as equally sized as possible
  2. Fill a deep non-stick pan with olive oil, turn on the stove at medium heat and add the potatoes once the oil is hot enough â€“ add them very carefully to prevent splashing of hot oil
  3. Cook the potatoes in the oil at medium-low heat for about 30 minutes
  4. Set aside the cooked potatoes and cover with tinfoil for 30 minutes
  5. Add your beaten eggs to the mixture, and then add it back into the pan on medium heat
  6. Cook for 4 minutes on one side, then use a plate bigger than the size of the pan to flip the tortilla and return it to the pan with the uncooked side facing down
  7. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, remove the tortilla using the same method and let it cool down for a couple of minutes
  8. Cut it into pieces and enjoy
